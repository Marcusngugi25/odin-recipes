Fruit and Yoghurt Smoothie

A simple and mostly healthy drink to sooth your tastebuds.

Time and Servings

  • Prep Time - 5 minutes
  • Total Time - 5 minutes
  • Servings - 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup of pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon of milk

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Serve and enjoy
